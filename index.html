Discover Winnipeg

The heart of Manitoba. Full of culture, history, and raw nature.

Culture Nature History

Culture

Winnipeg is known for its vibrant arts scene, with galleries, theaters, and festivals throughout the year. The city hosts the famous Winnipeg Folk Festival and the Royal Winnipeg Ballet, bringing world-class performances to local audiences.

Nature

Winnipeg sits at the meeting point of the Red and Assiniboine Rivers. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy The Forks, Assiniboine Park, and the sprawling nature trails. In winter, the city offers ice skating and snow activities for all ages.

History

Winnipeg has a rich history as a center of trade, settlement, and cultural exchange. From the historic Exchange District to the Manitoba Museum, visitors can explore the city's past and learn about the indigenous peoples and settlers who shaped it.
